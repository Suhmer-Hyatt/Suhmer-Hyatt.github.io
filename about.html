Who I am

My name is Suhmer Hyatt (pronounced like the season) and I'm a Toronto based illustrator. I think of illustration as accessible fine art. Art is my first love and more recently, it has allowed me to express my exploration of my identity as a female designer of colour. I would like you to come on this journey with me; even if we are on different journeys, I hope you enjoy the art and design I create along the way.
